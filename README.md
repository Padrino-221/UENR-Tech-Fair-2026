# 🌟 UENR Tech Fair 2026
**Building Tonight, Innovating Tomorrow.**

Welcome to the official repository for the **UENR Tech Fair 2026**. This high-fidelity, SaaS-inspired platform is designed to showcase student innovation and facilitate academic-industry engagement.

---

## 📅 Event Overview
- **Event Date**: June 26, 2026
- **Host**: Department of Information Technology and Decision Sciences (ITDS), UENR
- **Location**: UENR Campus, Sunyani, Ghana

The UENR Tech Fair is an annual technology gathering that has rapidly grown since 2021 into one of Ghana’s largest and most significant technology events, attracting thousands of participants.

---

## 🎯 Mission & Vision

### Mission
To empower the next generation of tech leaders by advancing tech education, fostering a culture of creativity and excellence, and bridging the gap between academic innovation and real-world application.

### Vision
To be the catalyst that transforms Ghana’s digital landscape. The fair envisions a future where digitalization is a fundamental driver of national growth, enhancing efficiency, transparency, and innovation across all sectors.

---

## ✨ Features
- **Modern SaaS Aesthetics**: A premium dark mode experience with glassmorphism, glowing accents, and smooth transitions.
- **Dynamic Countdown**: Tracking the seconds until the main event on June 26th.
- **Responsive Navigation**: Seamless experience on every device, from smartphone to desktop.
- **Innovation Hub**: Integrated platform for student innovators to showcase their projects.
- **Registration Flow**: Dedicated modal system for students, professionals, and exhibitors.

---

## 🛠️ Project Structure
- `index.html`: The high-impact landing page.
- `about.html`: Detailed history, mission, and vision.
- `committees.html`: The team behind the 2026 edition.
- `event.html`: Interactive timeline and schedule.
- `gallery.html`: Visual archive of innovation.
- `contact.html`: Global engagement portal.
- `SVG/`: Custom vector assets for branding.
- `style.css`: The comprehensive design system and SaaS styling.
- `script.js`: Interactive elements, custom cursor, and countdown logic.
